Smart Track Management System

1. Fleet Management for Logistics Companies

Use Case: Track trucks or delivery vans in real time, monitor routes, fuel usage, and delays.

Benefit: Enables dispatchers to optimize delivery routes, notify customers of ETAs, and handle delays proactively.

2. Student Attendance and Campus Movement Tracking

Use Case: Universities can track student check-ins across different locations (classrooms, library, labs).

Benefit: Ensures safety, automates attendance, and helps in managing facility usage.

3. Hospital Asset and Staff Tracking System

Use Case: Track location of critical equipment (like ventilators, wheelchairs) and medical staff across departments.

Benefit: Reduces time lost in searching for equipment, improves emergency response, and enhances operational efficiency.
